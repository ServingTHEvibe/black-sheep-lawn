# Black Sheep Lawn Care — Design System

## Brand Identity
Tech-Forward Minimalism + Glassmorphism. "Built Different" — elite residential lawn care in Des Moines.

## Colors
- **Primary Canvas:** #070906 (deep obsidian)
- **Background:** #111414
- **Spring Green (CTA):** #c1ff72
- **Surface containers:** #1d2020, #191c1c, #282a2b
- **Text primary:** #e1e3e3
- **Text muted:** #c6c7c0, #90918b
- **Champagne accent:** #BDBA9E
- **Glass card:** rgba(255,255,255,0.05) + backdrop-blur(12px)

## Typography
- **Display:** Sora 800, 72px, -0.04em tracking
- **Headlines:** Sora 700, 48px → 32px mobile
- **Body:** Inter 400/500, 16–18px
- **Labels/caps:** Space Mono 700, 12px, 0.1em tracking, ALL CAPS

## Layout
- Max container: 1280px
- Desktop margins: 64px
- Mobile margins: 20px
- Section gaps: 120px
- Grid: 12 col desktop, 8 tablet, 4 mobile

## Effects
- Noise texture overlay (2% opacity) on all dark surfaces
- Glassmorphism cards: bg rgba(255,255,255,0.05) + blur(12px) + 1px border rgba(255,255,255,0.1)
- Ambient green glow shadows: rgba(62,78,62,0.3)
- Hover: scale(1.02) on buttons, scale(1.05) on cards

## Sections
1. Nav — fixed glassmorphic bar
2. Hero — fullscreen video background with overlay + headline
3. Trusted By — logos marquee
4. Services bento grid
5. Gallery horizontal scroll
6. Pricing 3-tier
7. Testimonials
8. Contact form
9. Footer
